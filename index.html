Great Event Is Here

BrainWreck, an Intra college fest exclusively for the I-year students of GPREC, is conducted by III year coordinators under the guidance of IV years with the help of II years of Training and Placement Cell. The main aim of the week-long fest is to conduct ice-breaking activities for the freshman to get them to open up and adapt to the college life and environment. The games are an electric combination of entertainment and knowledge which put to test the student's quick wit, common sense and stage presence. Year after year, hordes of students register themselves for this 'Pre-Freshers' event and whilst having fun, also compete with each other to win the coveted 'Star of the Fest', a title given to the most proactive participant. Brain Wreck was started in 2011 and is continued as a legacy till now by Training and Placement cell coordinators. To know more about the Training and Placement club click below.
